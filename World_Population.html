There are over 48 million players in FFXIV, and to accomodate the large playerbase, the are several Data Centers (DCs) for different regions. Currently, there are 4 North American DCs, 4 Japanese DCs, 2 European DCs, and 1 DC for Oceania.

I want to focus on the North American game scene, so the below chart displays the number of active (subscription-paying) players over time.


The Data Centers with consistently high numbers of players are Crystal and Aether. Dynamis is a new Data Center, added on 12/31/2022. Active player numbers peaked in Spring of 2022. The COVID-19 Pandemic began around Spring of 2019, but it peaked around the months between 2021 and 2022. There is a noticeable increasing trend that began around this time, and it makes sense that the peak would come a few months later, when people have settled into remote life and have more time to explore games. Numbers dropped and stabilized around Fall of 2022, around the time where many returned back to regular life. Since people are no longer at home, they are less likely to have time to play the game and thus cancelled their subscription.

Overall, there is a healthy amount of players. An interesting note is that Aether and Primal are seen as the more "hardcore", raid-focused Data Centers of North America. In contrast, Crystal is seen as the more casual data center, with more of an emphasis on community-organized events and socializing with fellow players.
