Welcome!
As a long-time player of Final Fantasy 14 and overall curious person, I've always wondered about the people of this game. How many people play Dragoon? Which world has the most people? Is Crystal really that inactive in regards to raiding? On this site, I have compiled some graphs I made that can help me answer these questions!


To start, an overview of what Final Fantasy 14 is can be found on the About page. Then, an overview of player numbers across Data Centers and Worlds is on the World Populations page. Finally, a dive into how hardcore each World is can be found on the Savage Statistics page, where I look at how prevalent raiding is across Data centers.
